About

I am a Game Design and Development graduate from Macquarie University, with a passion for animation, and a deep interest in game design. I love playing video games since I was 7 years old, playing on almost all platforms, even recently getting into Virtual Reality!

I love experimenting with different types of animation in games, from Frame Animation to 3D, and constantly learning new game animation techniques. I am proficient in C# and the Unity game engine. I have also worked on various game projects throughout my degree, from a stickman platformer game, to an energetic VR experience for neuroscience and psychology.
I also run a gaming YouTube channel ranging from gameplay videos to reviews, although I'm planning to start producing video essays of game design in the future.
